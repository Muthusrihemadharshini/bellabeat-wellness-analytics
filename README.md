# 📊 Bellabeat Wellness Analytics Case Study

## 🚀 Overview
Analyzed smart device usage data (Fitbit dataset) to uncover patterns in user activity, sleep, and heart rate behavior, and translate these insights into actionable marketing strategies for Bellabeat.
---

## 🎯 Business Objective
Identify behavioral trends in:
- Physical activity  
- Sleep patterns  
- Calorie burn  

👉 Use these insights to support data-driven marketing and product decisions for Bellabeat.

---

## 📂 Dataset
- Fitbit user data (33 participants)  
- Includes:
  - Daily activity  
  - Sleep tracking  
  - Heart rate  
  - Hourly steps  
  - METs (energy expenditure)

---

## 🛠 Tools & Technologies
- R (tidyverse, ggplot2)  
- Data Cleaning & Transformation  
- Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)  
- Data Visualization  

---

## 🔍 Key Analysis Areas

### 📈 Activity vs Calories
- Strong positive correlation between steps and calories burned  
- Most users fall within moderate activity levels (0–15,000 steps)

💡 Insight:
Step tracking is a reliable indicator of energy expenditure.

---

### 🪑 Activity vs Sedentary Behavior
- Clear inverse relationship between steps and sedentary minutes  

💡 Insight:
Users with low activity levels form a high-potential segment for engagement.

---

### ❤️ Heart Rate Distribution
- Majority of users fall within normal resting BPM (60–90)  
- Some users show elevated heart rates (>100 BPM)

💡 Insight:
Opportunities for stress management and health monitoring features.

---

### 😴 Sleep vs Activity
- Slight negative correlation between sleep duration and steps  
- High-activity users show signs of recovery sleep patterns  

💡 Insight:
Balance between activity and rest is critical for overall wellness.

---

## 📊 Key Insights Summary
- Moderate activity dominates user behavior  
- High sedentary users present a major growth opportunity  
- Heart rate data enables personalized health insights  
- Sleep and activity are interconnected but influenced by lifestyle  

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

### 🎯 1. Target Inactive Users
- Introduce beginner-friendly walking programs  
- Send movement reminders and nudges  

---

### 📈 2. Engage Active Users
- Provide progress tracking and performance insights  
- Add gamification and reward systems  

---

### ❤️ 3. Health Monitoring Features
- Heart rate alerts for abnormal BPM  
- Stress-relief and mindfulness recommendations  

---

### 😴 4. Smart Sleep Coaching
- Recommend sleep schedules based on activity levels  
- Promote recovery tracking after high activity
